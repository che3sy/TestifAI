Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8/19/2024

Thank you for choosing our app! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Data Collection and Use

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. Free-response answers to quiz questions are stored anonymously using a randomly generated ID. These responses are used solely to enhance your experience and are automatically deleted after processing.

2. Google Gemini Integration

This app utilizes Google Gemini to generate AI-powered quizzes. While Google Gemini may process data for the purposes of generating quiz content, no personal or identifiable information is ever shared with or collected by us.

3. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously and implement strong security measures to ensure that any data processed is protected. All free-response data is stored temporarily and deleted immediately after use.

4. Your Rights

As we do not collect or retain any personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
